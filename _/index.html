About Afia Orbit

Who We Are

Afia Orbit is a tech-based waste management company that is transforming MSW (Municipal Solid Waste) into clean energy. We use various green technologies to process MSW into compressed biogas, quality recyclables, and methanol.

Our Vision

To build a zero-waste culture in Tanzania, creating a sustainable environment for future generations.

Our Mission

To deliver the technology, infrastructure, and solutions needed to foster a zero-waste culture in Tanzania.

Products & Services

AFIA Terminal

A waste management software platform catered to waste collectors/haulers with valid municipal contracts for waste collections to easily manage their operations.

Learn More

Compressed Biogas

We transform organic waste into compressed biogas (CBG) for cooking and transportation fuel, providing a clean energy alternative.

Quality Recyclables

Our material recovery facility collects, sorts, and recovers recyclable materials sold back to manufacturers for processing.

Methanol Production

Through pyrolysis, we convert unrecyclable materials into syngas that is used for heating, production of methanol, and electricity generation.

Stakeholder Collaboration

We work with industry stakeholders including LGAs, Waste Contractors, and Households to improve waste management in Dar Es Salaam, Tanzania.

Clean Energy Solutions

Our comprehensive approach turns waste problems into energy solutions, reducing landfill use and creating sustainable energy sources.
