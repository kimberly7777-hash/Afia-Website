About Afia Orbit

Who We Are

Afia Orbit is a tech-based waste management company that is transforming MSW (Municipal Solid Waste) into clean energy. We use various green technologies to process MSW into compressed biogas, quality recyclables, and methanol.

Our Vision

To build a zero-waste culture in Tanzania, creating a sustainable environment for future generations.

Our Mission

To deliver the technology, infrastructure, and solutions needed to foster a zero-waste culture in Tanzania.

Products & Services

AFIA Terminal

A waste management software platform catered to waste collectors/haulers with valid municipal contracts for waste collections to easily manage their operations, track routes, and optimize collection efficiency.

Visit Platform

Compressed Biogas

We transform organic waste into compressed biogas (CBG) for cooking and transportation fuel, providing a clean energy alternative that reduces carbon emissions and dependence on fossil fuels.

Quality Recyclables

Our material recovery facility collects, sorts, and recovers recyclable materials sold back to manufacturers for processing, creating a circular economy and reducing landfill waste.

Methanol Production

Through pyrolysis, we convert unrecyclable materials into syngas that is used for heating, production of methanol, and electricity generation, turning waste into valuable energy resources.

Stakeholder Collaboration

We work with industry stakeholders including LGAs, Waste Contractors, and Households to improve waste management in Dar Es Salaam, Tanzania through education, partnerships, and community engagement.

Clean Energy Solutions

Our comprehensive approach turns waste problems into energy solutions, reducing landfill use and creating sustainable energy sources that power homes, businesses, and communities.
